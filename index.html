Overview

Jim Corbett

Enjoy the raw beauty of nature in Jim Corbett National Park. Here you can find many wild animals roaming freely inside the deep jungle. Hop on thrilling safari adventures.

Rishikesh

Discover the spiritual vibes of Rishikesh, where the Ganga flows in the backdrop of the Himalayas. You can do yoga, and meditation and enjoy many adventure sports.

Ranthambore

Visit Ranthambore for exciting jungle safaris, which will give you a chance to spot elusive tigers along with other animals in their natural habitat. Explore the old forts and scenic landscapes..

Gir National Park

Think about the thrill of spotting Asiatic Lions in their own homes. Go on different safaris to explore the vast area filled with wild animals along with many bird species.

Udaipur

magine that you’ll have a great time in the “City of Lakes”, with its stunning palaces, vibrant bazaars, and romantic boat rides. Discover the rich history and charm of this royal destination

Somnath

Explore this sacred city that is home to the famous Somnath Temple. Enjoy the breathtaking sunsets near the Arabian Sea and soak into the spiritual aura of this famous pilgrimage site.

Nainital

This beautiful town is between the Himalayan Mountains, and various lakes surround it. Spend time by doing boating, trekking, and seeing the breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks.

Jaisalmer

Spend a great time in the golden hues and desert charm of Jaisalmer. This scenic place is known as the golden city of India with ancient forts, vibrant markets, and sand dunes.

Dwarka

A city steeped in mythology and spirituality, known for the famous temple Dwarkadish. Find peace and calmness in the divine ambiance of this pilgrimage destination.
